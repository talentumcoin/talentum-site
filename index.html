Talentum Features

  • Blockchain

    The Talentum blockchain is capable of handling higher transaction volume than its counterpart - Bitcoin. geting faster confirmation times.

  • Mining Reward

    Miners are currently awarded with 50 new talentus per block, an amount which gets halved roughly every 4 years.

  • Wallet Encryption

    Wallet encryption allows you to secure your wallet, so that you can view transactions and your account balance, but are required to enter your password before spending Talentus.

  • Security

    Talentum transactions are secured by military grade cryptography. You have control over your money and a strong level of protection against many types of fraud.

  • Peer-to-peer

    Peer-to-peer (P2P) Uses a decentralized network, is a distributed application architecture between peers. so no centralized third party to trust.

  • Fast international

    Send Talentus fast and easy. There are no banks to make you wait, no extra fees, and no special limitations on the minimum or maximum amount you can send.

Why Choose Talentum

Decentralize the economy.

For many years the world economy has been controlled by some, With Talentum we want to give access to financial resources to the less privileged. Decentralize the economy.

This is one reason why we offered to sell the first coins to as many people as possible instead of selling them to large investors. The more people buy the currency, the more people will benefit.

Strategic partnerships

Dentro das parcerias temos: Social Commerce, Sistema de Investimentos, Publicidad online e outros.

About Us

The name Talentum arises when remembering a very strong and important coin in the times of Jesus,
Talentum. (From ancient Greek: , talanton, meaning "scale", "scale") The talanton was a unit of ancient Mesopotamia for mass quantities, being raised in Sumer, and consolidated around the 23rd century BC. Antiquity with few variations of weight: Greece, Rome, Egypt, Israel, Babylon, Sumer and Acadia. Talent was already used in Mesopotamia and Israel, but it was from the introduction of talent in Ancient Greece and subsequent adaptation to the Roman system that this unity spread to the Mediterranean world.


Talentum is the currency of the new age.

Philosophy.
The world's economy has long been controlled by a few. The status quo has been shaped over the centuries, making humans engaged in rat races, damaging rivalry and bloodshed. Despite humanity's hope of overcoming local crises through education and internationalization, we still can not have complete control over our lives.
However, advances in technology, math, and cryptography can become the key to subverting this paradigm. The advent of cryptocurrencies is the first sign that the new world is coming. It is marked with the hope that the economy will intertwine with technology, that communities will establish new transparent principles and cryptographic algorithms will control its implementation.
Emancipation begins with the less privileged gaining access to financial resources that will give the oppressed hope for quality education, clean water, and a better life. Talentum represent the first small step to regain power over ourselves in order to live peacefully and prosper.
